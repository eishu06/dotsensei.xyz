Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Web3 TLD?

A Web3 Top Level Domain (TLD) is a blockchain-based domain name that gives you ownership, security, and utility across the decentralized web.

How do I mint a .SENSEI domain?

Simply click on MINT NOW and search for your desired name. Minting is quick and secure via Freename.io.

Why choose .SENSEI?

Because knowledge and authority deserve their own namespace. If you’re a guide, teacher, mentor, or innovator, this TLD is for you.

Is my domain permanent?

Yes. Unlike traditional DNS, Web3 domains are minted on-chain and remain yours forever without renewal fees.
