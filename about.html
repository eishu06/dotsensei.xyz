About .sensei

The word “SENSEI” means teacher, mentor, or guide. In the Web3 world, .SENSEI is a digital home for creators, educators, innovators, and leaders who want to share knowledge and build trust.

By minting a .SENSEI domain, you join a community that values wisdom, authenticity, and authority in the digital age.
